CAREER @ PENN — Sample Company Cards
======================================================================

HOW TO READ THESE CARDS:

Each card represents a company that hires Penn students. The data
combines two sources:
  1. Penn Career Services reports → WHICH companies hire from each school
  2. Levels.fyi crowd-sourced data → WHAT those companies pay interns

Penn stopped publishing internship data after 2019. For 2026 cards,
the Penn intern counts come from one of three sources:

  "Projected" = Company had actual Penn intern data in 2017-2019.
                The count is projected to 2026 using the overall trend
                in post-grad hiring from 2019 to 2024.

  "Inferred"  = Company appears in Penn post-grad hiring data (2024)
                but has NO intern-specific data. We assume they also
                take Penn interns, but the count is from post-grad hires.

  "Actual"    = Direct from Penn Career Services (2017-2019 only).

Salary data from Levels.fyi is always current and not projected.
